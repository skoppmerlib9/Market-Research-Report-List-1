<p><p>The Automotive Air Distribution System Market features primarily two types: Conventional Air Ducts and Twin-sheet Thermoformed Air Ducts. Conventional Air Ducts are traditional components made from rigid materials, designed to direct airflow efficiently within vehicle interiors. In contrast, Twin-sheet Thermoformed Air Ducts are produced through a specialized process that combines two sheets of thermoplastic to create lightweight, durable, and complex shapes. This modern approach enhances design flexibility and thermal efficiency, catering to the increasing demands for improved vehicle performance and comfort.</p></p>
