<p><p>The snowboard boots market consists of three main types: front-entry, rear-entry, and side-entry boots. Front-entry boots offer a traditional design with a single or dual lace system, providing excellent support and control. Rear-entry boots feature a hinged design that allows for easy entry and exit, making them user-friendly for beginners. Side-entry boots, less common, offer a unique lateral access design that combines features of the first two types, catering to specific preferences for comfort and convenience on the slopes.</p></p>
