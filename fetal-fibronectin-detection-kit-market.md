<p><p>The Fetal Fibronectin Detection Kit market encompasses two primary types: Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) and Colloidal Gold Method. ELISA is a highly sensitive laboratory technique that uses enzyme-linked antibodies to quantify fetal fibronectin levels, providing reliable diagnostic results. In contrast, the Colloidal Gold Method utilizes nanoparticles coated with antibodies for a rapid, user-friendly point-of-care test, yielding quick results. Both methods play crucial roles in predicting preterm labor, thus aiding healthcare providers in patient management decisions.</p></p>
