<p><strong>The Surface Mounted Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Mixed Process</li><li>Double-sided Mixed Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Mounted Technology (SMT) encompasses various market types based on assembly processes. The Single-sided Mixed Process involves assembling components on one side of the printed circuit board (PCB), allowing for simplified manufacturing with both surface-mounted and traditional components. In contrast, the Double-sided Mixed Process enables component placement on both sides of the PCB, maximizing space and functionality. This approach is often used for more complex designs, as it accommodates a larger array of components while maintaining efficiency in production.</p></p>
