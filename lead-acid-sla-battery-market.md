<p><strong>The Lead Acid (SLA) Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>GEL Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lead Acid Battery (SLA) market primarily comprises two types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, providing high power output, quick recharge, and resistance to vibration, making them ideal for applications like UPS and automotive. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica-based gel, offering enhanced safety, deep cycling capabilities, and better performance in extreme temperatures, making them suitable for renewable energy systems and emergency backup.</p></p>
