<p><p>Digital Audio Broadcasting (DAB) is a digital radio technology that delivers high-quality audio and additional data services. The market is primarily divided into two bands: the III Band and the L Band. The III Band, operating in the 174-240 MHz range, is widely used across Europe and offers numerous channels with better signal quality and reduced interference. The L Band, operating in the 1452-1492 MHz range, is utilized in several countries for niche applications and enhances coverage in urban areas.</p></p>
