<p><strong>The Fully Automated Immunoassay Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Analysis</li><li>Fluorescent Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fully Automated Immunoassay Analyzer market comprises three primary types: chemiluminescence analysis, fluorescent immunoassay, and radioimmunoassay. Chemiluminescence analysis uses chemical reactions to emit light, enhancing sensitivity and enabling the detection of low analyte concentrations. Fluorescent immunoassay employs fluorescent labels, allowing for precise measurements through the analysis of emitted light. Radioimmunoassay utilizes radioactive isotopes to quantify substances, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Each type caters to specific diagnostic and research needs, influencing their adoption across clinical laboratories.</p></p>
