<p><strong>The Solar LED Street Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Poly Crystalline</li><li>Mono Crystalline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar LED street lighting market primarily features two types of solar panels: polycrystalline and monocrystalline. Polycrystalline panels, made from multiple silicon crystals, are often more affordable and easier to produce, making them a popular choice for budget-conscious applications. Monocrystalline panels, composed of a single crystal structure, are more efficient and take up less space, delivering higher energy output. As a result, they are favored for areas where space is limited or higher efficiency is crucial, despite their higher cost.</p></p>
