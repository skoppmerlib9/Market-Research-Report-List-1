<p><ul><li>Output Transformer</li><li>Input Transformer</li><li>Interstage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio transformers market encompasses three primary types: output transformers, input transformers, and interstage transformers. Output transformers are essential for matching impedance between audio sources and speakers, ensuring signal integrity. Input transformers help in balancing audio signals, thereby reducing noise and interference, especially in professional audio settings. Interstage transformers are used to connect different stages of amplification within devices, optimizing signal transfer and fidelity. Together, these transformers play a crucial role in enhancing audio quality and performance in various applications.</p></p>
