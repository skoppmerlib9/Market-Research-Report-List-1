<p><p>The artificial barrier casings market comprises various types that cater to different processing needs. Collagen casings are derived from animal collagen, offering strength and flexibility. Cellulose casings are made from plant fibers, ideal for skinless sausages. Plastic casings provide durability and moisture barrier properties. Fibrous casings, often made from natural fibers, are used for smoked sausages. Value-added casings include enhancements like flavoring or color, while textile casings are designed for specialized applications. Net casings are used for traditional presentations and aesthetics.</p></p>
