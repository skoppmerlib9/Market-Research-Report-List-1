<p><strong>The Dual Access Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Distal Radiopaque Markers Spaced</li><li>Single Radiopaque Marker At Distal Tip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dual Access Catheter market comprises two main types: those with two distal radiopaque markers spaced along the catheter and those with a single radiopaque marker at the distal tip. The two-marker design allows for enhanced visibility and precise placement within the vascular system, aiding in complex procedures. Conversely, the single marker option simplifies visualization at the tip, making it suitable for less intricate interventions. Both designs cater to specific clinical needs, enhancing procedural accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
