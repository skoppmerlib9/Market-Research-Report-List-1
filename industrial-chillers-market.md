<p><ul><li>Air Chillers</li><li>Water Chillers</li><li>Evaporative Condensed Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial chillers market comprises three main types: air chillers, water chillers, and evaporative condensed chillers. Air chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them energy-efficient and suitable for small to medium applications. Water chillers utilize water as a cooling medium, providing better efficiency for larger systems. Evaporative condensed chillers combine air and water cooling techniques, enhancing performance in hot climates. Each type serves diverse industries, ensuring optimal temperature control for processes and equipment.</p></p>
