<p><p>The paint robots market is categorized into three primary types based on their mounting configurations. Floor-mounted robots are positioned on the ground, providing stability and flexibility for various painting tasks. Ceiling-mounted robots are suspended from overhead structures, optimizing space and allowing for efficient coverage on large surfaces. Rail-mounted robots, on the other hand, move along a fixed track, enabling precise and consistent painting across lengthy or expansive areas. Each type serves different operational needs in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
