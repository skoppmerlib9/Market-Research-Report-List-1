<p><p>The wheel bearing market comprises various types, including Generation 1, Generation 2, Generation 3, and Others. Generation 1 bearings are conventional designs, mostly using tapered rollers, while Generation 2 bearings incorporate sealed technology for improved performance. Generation 3 bearings feature a more advanced design, often integrating sensors for real-time monitoring. "Others" refers to specialized or niche bearings tailored for specific applications. Each type is engineered to enhance durability, reduce friction, and improve overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
