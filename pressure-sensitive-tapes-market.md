<p><p>The pressure-sensitive tape market encompasses various types, each designed for specific applications. Single coated tape has adhesive on one side, ideal for attaching objects. Double coated tape features adhesive on both sides, perfect for bonding surfaces together. Adhesive transfer tape consists of just the adhesive on a backing, allowing for versatile use in labeling and mounting. Self wound tape lacks a carrier, allowing for direct application without backing, commonly used in insulation and sealing. Each type serves distinct needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
