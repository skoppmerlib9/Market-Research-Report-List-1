<p><p>The Follicle Stimulating Hormone (FSH) Test Kit market comprises two main types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). CLIA utilizes a chemical reaction to produce light, enabling precise measurement of FSH levels in biological samples. In contrast, TRIA employs time-resolved fluorescence, enhancing sensitivity through delayed signal detection. Both methods are vital for assessing reproductive health, diagnosing disorders, and monitoring fertility treatments, contributing significantly to the growing diagnostics market.</p></p>
