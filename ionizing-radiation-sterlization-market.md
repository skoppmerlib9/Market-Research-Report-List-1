<p><p>The ionizing radiation sterilization market comprises three main types: electron beams, X-rays, and gamma rays. Electron beams provide rapid processing with a high dose rate, suitable for medical devices and heat-sensitive products. X-rays penetrate deeper, allowing for sterilization of dense or bulky items, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals and food packaging. Gamma rays, originating from radioactive isotopes, are widely used for their extensive penetration and consistent sterilization of various materials, including healthcare products and industrial applications.</p></p>
