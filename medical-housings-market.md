<p><strong>The Medical Housings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Forming</li><li>Pressure Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical housings market involves two primary types: vacuum forming and pressure forming. Vacuum forming utilizes heat to soften a thermoplastic sheet, which is then molded over a fixture by applying a vacuum, creating lightweight and customizable components. Pressure forming involves similar heating but uses positive air pressure to shape the material, allowing for more intricate designs and sharper details. Both processes are essential for producing durable, sterile housing for medical equipment, ensuring safety and functionality in healthcare applications.</p></p>
