<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive backup camera lens market primarily consists of two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer superior image quality, low noise, and better performance in low-light conditions, making them suitable for high-end vehicles. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more power-efficient, cost-effective, and increasingly used in modern vehicles due to advancements in technology. Both types enhance driver safety by providing clear rear-view images, facilitating parking and maneuvering.</p></p>
