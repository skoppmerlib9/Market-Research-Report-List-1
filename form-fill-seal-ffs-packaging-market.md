<p><p>Form-Fill-Seal (FFS) packaging involves automated machines that create packages from a flat roll of film, fill them with products, and seal them in a continuous process. The market is primarily divided into two types: vertical FFS and horizontal FFS. Vertical type is suitable for granular or powdered products, utilizing space efficiently while occupying a smaller footprint. In contrast, horizontal type is ideal for larger, bulk items and often offers higher sealing speeds, making it suitable for diverse packaging applications.</p></p>
