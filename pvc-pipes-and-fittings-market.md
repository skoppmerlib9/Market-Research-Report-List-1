<p><p>PVC pipes and fittings are categorized into three main types based on their shapes and applications: K Type, L Type, and M Type. K Type pipes are thicker and designed for high-pressure systems, making them suitable for industrial applications. L Type pipes are medium-thickness, ideal for general plumbing and drainage. M Type pipes are the thinnest, typically used for less demanding applications like irrigation. Each type caters to specific needs, ensuring efficient performance in various construction and plumbing projects.</p></p>
