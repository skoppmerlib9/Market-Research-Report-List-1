<p><ul><li>Power Steering</li><li>Manual Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus steering systems market comprises two main types: power steering and manual steering. Power steering utilizes hydraulic or electric actuators to assist the driver in turning the wheels, enhancing maneuverability and reducing driver fatigue, especially in larger vehicles. In contrast, manual steering relies solely on the driver’s physical effort to turn the steering wheel, offering simplicity and lower cost. Both systems cater to different bus applications, with power steering increasingly preferred for its ease of use and improved driving experience.</p></p>
