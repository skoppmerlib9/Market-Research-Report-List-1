<p><ul><li>Metal-halide Lamp Type</li><li>LED Lamp Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floodlighting market encompasses two primary types: metal-halide lamps and LED lamps. Metal-halide lamps are traditional high-intensity discharge lamps known for their bright, white light and are commonly used in sports facilities and outdoor areas. However, they are less energy-efficient and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED lamps are gaining popularity due to their energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower heat emission, making them ideal for various applications, including architectural lighting and security purposes.</p></p>
