<p><strong>The Portable Automated External Defibrillators AEDs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Automated External Defibrillators</li><li>Fully Automated External Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market includes two main types: Semi-Automated External Defibrillators and Fully Automated External Defibrillators. Semi-Automated AEDs provide voice prompts and require the user to deliver a shock after assessing the heart rhythm, making them user-friendly for non-medical responders. In contrast, Fully Automated AEDs analyze the heart rhythm and deliver shocks automatically without user intervention. Both types are crucial for responding to cardiac emergencies outside of clinical settings, enhancing survival rates in critical situations.</p></p>
