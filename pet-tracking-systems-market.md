<p><p>The pet tracking systems market encompasses various technologies to monitor pets' locations. GPS pet tracking systems use satellite signals for real-time tracking over large distances, ideal for outdoor pets. WiFi pet tracking systems rely on local wireless networks, offering precise tracking within a limited range, useful for indoor environments or urban settings. Radio pet tracking systems utilize radio frequency signals, providing reliable tracking even in remote areas, though with limited range compared to GPS. Each system caters to different tracking needs and environments.</p></p>
