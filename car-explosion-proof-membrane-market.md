<p><p>The car explosion-proof membrane market features two primary types: heat absorption film and reflective insulation film. Heat absorption films are designed to absorb and dissipate heat from explosions, enhancing passenger safety by preventing temperature spikes. Reflective insulation films, on the other hand, reflect thermal energy away from the vehicle's interior, helping to mitigate heat buildup during an explosion. Both types enhance vehicle resilience and safety, catering to the increasing demand for protective automotive technologies.</p></p>
