<p><p>The Polybenzimidazole (PBI) fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: PBI filament and PBI staple fiber. PBI filament is characterized by its continuous length, making it suitable for applications requiring high strength and durability, such as protective clothing and industrial fabrics. On the other hand, PBI staple fiber consists of shorter lengths, typically used in non-woven fabrics and insulation materials. Both types are renowned for their exceptional thermal stability and flame resistance, catering to specialized industrial and safety applications.</p></p>
