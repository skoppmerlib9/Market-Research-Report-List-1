<p><p>The stretch forming machine market is primarily divided into two types: sheet stretch forming machines and extrusion stretch forming machines. Sheet stretch forming machines are designed to shape flat metal sheets by stretching them over a mold, ideal for producing complex geometries in lightweight components. In contrast, extrusion stretch forming machines focus on elongating and shaping extruded materials, often used for structural components in various industries. Both types enhance efficiency in manufacturing by creating precise, durable parts tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
