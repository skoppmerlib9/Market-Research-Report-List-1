<p><strong>The Windshield Wiper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wiper Blade</li><li>Wiper Arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The windshield wiper market comprises two main types: wiper blades and wiper arms. Wiper blades are the rubber components that make contact with the windshield, providing visibility by removing rain, snow, or debris. They come in various types, including traditional, beam, and hybrid blades catering to different vehicle needs. Wiper arms, on the other hand, are the mechanical structures that hold the blades in place and facilitate their movement across the windshield. Together, they ensure effective windshield cleaning, enhancing driver safety.</p></p>
